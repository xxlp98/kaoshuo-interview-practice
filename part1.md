## (Part 1)

### 1. The area you live in 居住地

1. Where do you live? Do you like the area that you live in?
   你住在哪里？你喜欢自己住的地区吗？
2. What are some changes in the area recently?
   最近那个地区有什么变化吗？
3. Do you know any people live in this area?
   你知道有什么人住在这个地区吗？
4. Where do you like to go in that area?
   你喜欢去那个地区的哪里？
5. What part do you love most in the place?
   你最喜欢那个地区的什么部分？
6. Would you like to live in somewhere with completely different environmental conditions?
   你会想住在其他环境因素完全不同的地点吗？

### 2. Hometown 家乡

1. Where is your hometown? Is that a big city or a small place?
   你的家乡在哪里？是大城市还是小地方？
2. How long have you lived there?
   你在那里生活了多久？
3. What do you like the most about your hometown?
   你最喜欢家乡的什么？
4. Is there anything you dislike about it?
   你不喜欢那里的什么？
5. Do you know the history of your hometown?
   你知道家乡的历史吗？
6. Will you learn the history of your hometown?/Do the schools teach the history of your hometown?
   你会学习家乡的历史吗？/学校会教你们家乡的历史吗？
7. What is the most famous in your hometown?
   你们家乡最出名的是什么？

### 3. Accommodation 住所

1. Do you live in the house or apartment?
   你住在房子里还是公寓里？
2. Which is your favourite room in your home?
   你家里最喜欢的房间是哪个？
3. What will make your home more comfortable/happy?
   什么东西会让你的家更舒适/快乐？
4. How long have you live there?
   你在那里住了多久？
5. Do you live along or with others?
   你自己住还是和别人住？
6. Do you plan to live there for a long time?
   你打算在那住很久吗？
7. What do you want to change in the home?
   你想改变家里的什么？

### 4. Work or Study 工作/学习

#### Study:

1. What is your major? Why did you choose that subject?
   你的专业是什么？为什么会选择这个专业？
2. What is the major about? Do you like it?
   这个专业讲的是什么？你喜欢这个专业吗？
3. Do you think it is difficult? Why?
   你认为你的专业难吗？为什么？
4. What do you do after classes?
   你课后会做什么？
5. Do you prefer to study in the morning or in the afternoon?
   你更喜欢早上学习还是下午？
6. Are you looking forward to working?
   你期待进入职场吗？
7. Do you sometimes feel that you have to study too hard?
   你有时候会觉得自己学得太努力了吗？

#### Work:

1. What work do you do?
   你是做什么工作的？
2. Why did you choose to do that type of work?
   为什么你会选择这类工作？
3. Do you miss being a student?
   你想念学生时代吗？
4. Do you like your job?
   你喜欢自己的工作吗？
5. Is your job connect to your college major?
   你的工作和大学专业关联性强吗？
6. Do you prefer to work in the morning or in the afternoon?
   你更喜欢早上工作还是下午？
